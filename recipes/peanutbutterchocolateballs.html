Peanut Butter Chocolate Balls

Home Chocolate Balls filled with peanutbutter or almond butter

Ingredients

  • 75% chocolate bar or Dark chocolate chips
  • Peanut butter or almond butter
  • Confectionairy Sugar
  • Butter half a tea spoon
  • Sprinkles and or chocolate syrup left over for decoration and texture

Instructions

  1. mix the peanut butter with the powdered sugar, butter and form balls.
  2. Place in freezer to get cold fast, then melt the chocolate.
  3. After pour the melted chocolate over the balls and place your decorations on it quick or mix in the melted chocolate.
