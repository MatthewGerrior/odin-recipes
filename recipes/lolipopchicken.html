Lolipop chicken

Recipe Information

An appetizer that can be enjoyed as is or with chutney

Ingredients

  • Chicken legs
  • Marinade: 2 table spoons yogurt or Grk yogurt , Red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon garam masala, salt
  • Coating: 1 cup all purpose flour, 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 1 teaspoon, red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon garah masala, salt to taste, water as needed to make thick batter

Instructions

  1. Coat chicken in seasoning, Then marinade for 2 - 3 hours.
  2. Batter up wings and legs with coating mixture and cook.
  3. Fry or bake wings & legs for 20 - 35 minutes depending on chicken size.
