Red vabrant broth with rice cakes, fish cake and egg

recipe information

Ingredients

  • Vegetable broth
  • Korean Chilli powder 1 - 2 table spoons
  • Korean hot pepper paste teaspoon - 2 table spoons
  • Rice Noodle 1 pack of 10 - 20 or more
  • Fish cake 4 - 5 fish cakes
  • Scallions 3 4 inch pieces
  • Kombu Seaweed
  • egg 1 - 2 egg

Instructions

  1. Heat pot of vegetable broth till it starts to boil. simmer and add korean hot pepper paste, chilli powder, now stir.
  2. then add rice noodles and fish cake
  3. When Rice noodle and fish cake is cooked add scallions and kombu and simmer for 2-3 min
  4. Add egg hard boiled runny yolk or fried egg.
